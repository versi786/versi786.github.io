Aasif Versi is a software engineer living in New York, NY. He graduated from the **University of Pennsylvania** School of Engineering and Applied Sciences in 2017 with a B.S.E. in Computer Engineering. He currently works as a software engineer at **Citadel** on the Data Team within the Fixed Income and Macro fund. He also worked within the Fixed Income and Macro fund and the Central Risk Platform Engineering team on the Risk & PnL Infrastructure. Previously he worked as a software engineer at **Bloomberg LP** working on Real-Time Market Data Distribution.
